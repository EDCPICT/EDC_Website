Startup and Innovation Cell

ABOUT SIC

PICT SIC was inaugurated on 26th January, 2019.The Cell provides a platform to ideas which have great potential to become successful products or Services. Products/Services should solve the existing Social Issues. Main objective of the cell is to promote Project based learning and helping selected ideas to lead towards Startup and Entrepreneurship. The cell provides necessary infrastructure with Expert guidance by the faculty mentors and Industry experts. The cell has separate infrastructure for this cell and is accessible to students 24×7 with all necessary resources. The institute has reserved separate fund to support students to build product and service which is beneficial to the society.

Achievments

  1. "Sanitary Pad Vending Machine” was one of the project under this cell which is developed as a product according to the industrial standard and social need.
  2. This product was displayed at “Google’s developers Group Summit 2019” which was held at JW Marriott on 28th September. The girls who made this product were also interviewed by one of the agencies and their video has been put on YouTube as “Women’s Entrepreneurs from Pune”. The video till date got 5155 views and increasing.
  3. The same product also received order from “Rotary Club of Khadki” to deploy this machine in the government’s school of Khopoli.
  4. The “Sanitary Pad Vending Machine” is currently collaborated with “Bird Vision Consulting Pvt Ltd” to take this product to a next level.
  5. The another project from this cell titled “AI based Smart Traffic Junction” also got selected for presentation at “Google’s Developer Group Summit 2019” .
  6. “One College One App” was also the product from this cell which will be implemented as a fully fledged service from December onwards at PICT.
