"The Original Pitch"

Step in with an idea, Step out with a Startup...


The Original Pitch is one of the Pune’s Biggest Pitching Event organised by PICT EDC and Navyuvak Entrepreneurs. The Original Pitch is an event which encourages the young mind to brainstorm toward real life problem exist around them through their Innovative thinking. This event will give a platform to all such young minds and encourage them to continue to innovate and contribute to the ecosystem of innovation.

THREE PHASES:

1. Actionable Entrepreneurship Program

A 15 days workshop to provide knowledge from the grassroots level to advanced level about startups.
27th October – 9th November

2. Innovation Challenge – Original Pitch

Every participant can submit their video pitch individually or in a group (max 4 members). Top 20 teams will be selected and providing 2 weeks rigorous mentoring to convert into a startup. Final winners will be given ideation funding. Total prizes upto Rs 20 Lakhs

3. Virtual Incubation Program

3 months Program Every Wednesday – Hackathons Every Friday – Entrepreneur Meet
16th December – 15th March


PICT EDC

    The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) aims to create an environment where students get complete exposure to the Entrepreneurial ecosystem.
    PICT has always nurtured and helped entrepreneurs to successfully convert their small venture to soaring heights.
    For strengthening the entrepreneurial attributes of the budding professionals, EDC conducts motivational session from highly influential personalities who have set a benchmark in their field, so that the students get the right insight into the whole process.
    Aspiring students get the complete support and mentoring from the cell which forces them to develop a bigger dream and vision.

Navyuvak Entrepreneurs

    NAVYUVAK ENTREPRENEURS are the only organization In India to grant Equity Funding worth INR 65 lakh to ideation stage student startups.
    Initiated India's Largest Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive in Tier II and Tier III Cities with over 7000+ students, starting in Nagpur.
    India's only organization focusing on entrepreneurial education at price of INR 600 monthly, training- how to build a great startup idea!
    India's first and only organization to launch a Startup program for teachers solely focused on training academicians to start startups.
