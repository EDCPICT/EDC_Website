Business Incubation Center

PICT Business Incubation Center established in 2009. We provide Space, Infrastructure, electricity, Internet connectivity, office furniture, and technical support to incubate a startup company for period of maximum 2 years. We have incubated 19 companies so far. Some of them have successfully moved out to their own premises. We are proud of them.

"ENTREPRENEURSHIP is living a few years of your life like most people won't, so that you can spend the rest of your life like most people can't."

The business incubation center is a platform where we provide guidance for the business startups and also provide various services and facilities like high configuration systems, high speed internet, office space, and other resources etc.
