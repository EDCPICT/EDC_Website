MoU’s signed:

MoU signed between PICT & SecureThings U.S.,Inc.

MoU was signed between SecureThings , a silicon valley based US company and PICT for Automotive Security Research . 5 students from PICT are currently working with SecureThings on research problems based on Automotive Seucurity SecureThings has provided to PICT the “Automotive Testbench” to do the Experimentation and Analysis.

MoU signed between PICT & CYPHERROCK.

A MoU was signed between PICT and Cypherock for developing a dedicated research and applications in the field on Blockchain and Cryptocurrency. Also, the cell is assisting Cypherock in the field of Hardware security.

MoU signed between PICT & Navyuvak Entrepreneurs Pvt. Ltd.

Navyuvak Entrepreneurs Pvt. Ltd. has signed an MoU with PICT and has offered a working capital for the budding startups and entrepreneurs of the college. A capital of Rs. 50 lakh will be invested in potential startup ideas coming out of PICT.
