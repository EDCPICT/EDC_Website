Institution's Innovation Council

IIC

Institution's Innovation Council 2019 for Students & Faculty

MHRD’s Institution’s Innovation Center is established at SCTR’s Pune Institute of Computer Technology with consideration of various functions and benefits.



IIC Innovation Ambassador

The innovative and one-of-its-kind training program aims at training for faculties in innovation, entrepreneurship, Intellectual Property Rights (IPR), design thinking, product development, idea generation, among others. It will make the faculties, change-agents and ambassadors of innovation to make our students future-ready.

Objectives

Functions of Institution’s Innovation Council

  • To promote innovation ecosystem in institute.

  • To conduct innovation and entrepreneurship related activities as per guideline provided by MHRD.

  • To organize seminars, workshops, interaction related to IPR, entrepreneur talk to motivate student entrepreneur.

  • To organize activities for students like project/idea competition, create awareness of different government schemes available for student entrepreneurs.

Benefits to Institute, Faculty members and Students

  • Students/Faculties associated with IIC get an opportunity to participate and explore their ideas in events organized by MHRD.

  • Opportunity to meet Business leaders.

  • Opportunity to nurture business idea and convert it into prototype by getting seed funding by various agencies.

  • Mentorship by industry professionals.
